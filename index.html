My name is Andrew Guzman, I'm a first-generation Mexican-American living in California. My favorite hobby is going for hikes. I especially enjoy hiking in nature as it's a nice way to get away from the city. I appreciate this pastime because I get to spend time in nature while exercising my body which is beneficial for my mental health. The mental and physical health benefits of this activity are what makes it my favorite activity.

Hiking is a very easy hobby to get started with, but there are some pieces of advice you should follow to ensure it's a good experience for everybody involved. First, I would pick hikes that are geared towards beginners to make sure you don't over-exert yourself which can be dangerous without proper preparation. Second, make sure you invest in appropriate hiking gear. Hiking is a physical activity after all, so you want to be sure to wear appropriate clothing to make sure you are comfortable. Third, always make sure you tell someone you trust about where you will be to make sure you are safe. And finally, clean up after yourself and leave no trace behind!
An additional tip I have is to remember to pack enough water and snacks for the duration of your hike. This one is almost a non-negotiable as it can be extremely dangerous if you were to get lost somewhere without food and water. If you like using mobile apps, you can try out AllTrails which has thousands of guides and maps at your disposal. This one is especially helpful as it allows you to filter hikes based on difficulty, length, and time.
